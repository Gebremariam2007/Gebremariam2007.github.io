  • Project 2

    Creating an Eritrean krar from scratch was an incredibly rewarding experience for me. As I delved into the process, I discovered the intricate craftsmanship and cultural significance behind this traditional instrument. From selecting the wood to shaping the body and strings, every step allowed me to connect deeply with Eritrean heritage. The moment I strummed the first notes, I felt a sense of accomplishment and pride, knowing that I had not only built an instrument but also a bridge to a rich musical tradition. It was truly a journey of creativity and self-discovery..
