Hello, I'm Abel Gebremariam

Welcome to my personal website! In this website, you will learn about my interests, hobbies, and future goals.

I am originally from Eritrea, a small yet beautiful country located in the Horn of Africa. In 2017, I made the life-changing decision to move to the United States in pursuit of new opportunities and a brighter future. This transition allowed me to embrace a new culture, meet people from diverse backgrounds, and expand my horizons both personally and professionally. Though the journey has had its challenges, it has also been incredibly rewarding. My experiences in both Eritrea and the U.S. have shaped my values, giving me a unique perspective on the world and fueling my passion for growth, learning, and contributing to my community.
